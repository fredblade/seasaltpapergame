Sea Salt & Paper Reference Guide

Turn Options

On your turn, you must perform **one** of:

  • Draw 2, Keep 1: Draw the top two cards of the deck. Keep one, and discard the other onto one of the two discard piles.
  • Take top Discard: Take the top face-up card from either discard pile into your hand.

Then, you may play pairs of Duo cards to trigger their effects, and optionally call to end the round if you have at least 7 points.

Duo Cards (1 Point per Pair)

🦀 + 🦀 Crab Duo

Effect: Search either discard pile and add one card of your choice to your hand.

⛵ + ⛵ Boat Duo

Effect: Take an extra turn immediately.

🐟 + 🐟 Fish Duo

Effect: Draw the top card from the deck directly to your hand.

🦈 + 🏊 Shark + Swimmer Duo

Effect: Steal one random card from an opponent's hand.

Point Multipliers

🏠💡 Lighthouse

Scores +1 point for each Boat card you hold at round end.

🐠🐟 Shoal of Fish

Scores +1 point for each Fish card you hold at round end.

🐧🐧🐧 Penguin Colony

Scores +2 points for each Penguin card you hold at round end.

👨‍✈️ Captain

Scores +3 points for each Sailor card you hold at round end.

Special: Mermaid Card (🧜‍♀️)

  • Scoring: Grants 1 point per card of your most frequent color. If you hold multiple Mermaids, they apply to your next largest color sets respectively.
  • Four Mermaid Win: If you collect all 4 Mermaid cards, you win the entire match immediately!

STOP vs. LAST CHANCE

  • STOP: Round ends instantly. All players score their card points.
  • LAST CHANCE: Opponents get one final turn. If the caller holds the highest (or tied highest) score, they score card points + color bonus (1pt per card of largest color set), and opponents score ONLY color bonus. If the caller loses, opponents score cards, and the caller scores ONLY color bonus.
